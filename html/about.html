The Face Behind The Code

My name is Nausheen Salauddin and I am currently enrolled in the two-year diploma program in Web Development and Internet Applications at St. Clair College, Canada. I hold a bachelor's degree in computer science from Jain University, India, which I obtained in 2017.

In 2019, I moved to Singapore and attended an intensive three-month bootcamp in software engineering at General Assembly, where I obtained hands-on training in various technology stacks for frontend, backend, and full stack web development. I am also a certified UX Designer, having received certification from both General Assembly and Nielsen Norman Group.

Subsequently, I interned as a Junior Software Engineer at a start-up company in Singapore, where I was involved in designing the company's internal design framework and other projects. Afterward, I took up a job as an Instructional Associate at General Assembly, teaching Web Development. Presently, I am a peer tutor at St. Clair College.

Upon graduating from St. Clair College, I am keen to work in the field of Web Development. My interest lies in UX Development, yet I am open to exploring other areas within the scope of Software Engineering.

I am a curious person with an appetite for learning and growth and I strive to stay up-to-date with the current developments in the field and to continually enhance my abilities.
